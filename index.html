Rushed Chance Calculator

Enter your Wit stat to find out your percentage chance of being rushed (Ie.Totally Cooked) depending your Wit.

Formula
(6.5 / log10(0.1 * wiz + 1))² %

Notes:

  • Formula used: RushedChance = (6.5 / log10(0.1*WizStat + 1))² (result expressed as percentage).
  • If log10(0.1*wiz + 1) = 0 (e.g. wiz = 0), the result is undefined; please enter wit > 0.
  • The displayed percentage is capped at 100% since it cannot exceed that value.
